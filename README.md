**Unofficial Monkeytype Year in Review**

Monkeywrap is a fun, community-built project that generates a Spotify Wrapped-style summary of your typing statistics from [Monkeytype](https://monkeytype.com).

## Disclaimer
This project is **not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to Monkeytype** in any official capacity. It is a fan-made creation built to celebrate the community's typing achievements.

## Features
- **Personalized Stats**: Connects to your Monkeytype profile to visualize your WPM, accuracy, and consistency.
- **Interactive Slides**: Experience your year in typing through engaging, interactive cards.
- **Theme Support**: Switch between your favorite themes (Default, Serika Dark, Carbon, Botanical).
- **Shareable**: Easily share your wrap with friends.

## Tech Stack
- **Framework**: [Next.js](https://nextjs.org/) (App Router)
- **Styling**: [Tailwind CSS](https://tailwindcss.com/)
- **Animations**: [Framer Motion](https://www.framer.com/motion/)
- **Icons**: [Lucide React](https://lucide.dev/)
